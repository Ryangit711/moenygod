# Master Reference List

All books, papers, and resources referenced across this wiki. Organized by category.

## I. Money, Debt, and Monetary History

| # | Title | Author | Year | Why It Matters |
|---|-------|--------|------|----------------|
| 1 | *Debt: The First 5000 Years* | David Graeber | 2011 | The definitive anthropological takedown of the barter myth. Shows that credit and debt preceded money, and that debt has always been a tool of power. |
| 2 | *...and Forgive Them Their Debts: Lending, Foreclosure and Redemption from Bronze Age Finance to the Jubilee Year* | Michael Hudson | 2018 | How ancient societies used debt jubilees to prevent collapse. The origin of "forgive us our debts." |
| 3 | *The Ascent of Money: A Financial History of the World* | Niall Ferguson | 2008 | Broad history of financial innovation — bonds, stocks, insurance, real estate, hedge funds. |
| 4 | *A History of Money and Banking in the United States* | Murray Rothbard | 2002 | How the US banking system evolved, who designed it, and who benefited. |
| 5 | *The Lost Science of Money* | Stephen Zarlenga | 2002 | Traces the evolution of monetary systems and argues that money creation should be a public function. |
| 6 | *Money: Whence It Came, Where It Went* | John Kenneth Galbraith | 1975 | Witty, accessible history of money from ancient times to the modern era. |
| 7 | *The Babylonian Wergild Tradition* | Various scholars | — | Original clay tablet records of early debt contracts in Mesopotamia. |
